How to Play

The goal is to guess the 'common denominator' in a list of up to four items.

For Example, for clues,

  1. Nautilus
  2. Cockle
  3. Cawrie
  4. Conch

the Quadegory is Shells.

Clicking this button reveals the next word in the Quadegory's set.

Clicking this button reveals the answer. It'll only be enabled once all clues are revealed.

Tips and Tricks:

  • Clicking Shift reveals the next clue.
  • Clicking Enter submits your guess.
  • The last clue is always the closest to the answer.
  • Playing Quadegories improves your brain's executive function. Which means you're getting smarter!
