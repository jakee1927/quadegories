How to Play

The goal is to guess the 'common denominator' in a list of up to four items.

For Example, for clues,

  1. Nautilus
  2. Cockle
  3. Cawrie
  4. Conch

the Quadegory is Shells.

Clicking the Next Clue button reveals more information. First, it shows additional clues, then it reveals the first few letters of the answer.

Clicking the I'm Stuck! button reveals the answer. It'll only be enabled once all clues are revealed.

The blurred answer shows you the structure of the quadegory. As you reveal more clues, you'll also get to see the first few letters of the answer.

Tips and Tricks:

  • Click directly on the answer box to type your guess.
  • Pressing the Tab key reveals the next clue, just like clicking the Next Clue button.
  • Pressing Enter submits your guess.
  • The button color changes from green to red as you reveal more clues.
  • After revealing all clues, you'll get to see the first three letters of the answer.
  • The last clue is always the closest to the answer.
  • Playing Quadegories improves your brain's executive function. Which means you're getting smarter!
